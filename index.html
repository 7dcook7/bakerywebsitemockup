Signature Multigrain Rolls

Golden-brown, pillowy, and generously topped with a toasted seed blend, our Signature Multigrain Rolls are the heart of every meal. We bake each batch daily using traditional slow-fermentation methods, ensuring a soft, airy interior that perfectly contrasts the crunch of flax and sunflower seeds. Whether you’re looking for the perfect base for a gourmet slider or a warm, buttered accompaniment to a winter soup, these rolls offer a rustic, artisanal touch that tastes just like home.
